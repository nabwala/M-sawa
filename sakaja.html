Hon. Sakaja Johnson

Governor, Nairobi County (2022 – Present)

About

Hon. Sakaja Johnson currently serves as the Governor of Nairobi County, a position he has held since 2022. Known for his progressive urban policies, he champions public health, youth inclusion, and transparent governance. Prior to this, he served as the Senator for Nairobi and played a leading role in formulating youth policies at the national level.

Key Projects During Tenure

  • Korogocho Level 4 Hospital Construction
  • Nyayo Stadium Renovation
  • Lang'ata Market Modernizarion
  • Eatlands Digital Library
  • Westlands Fire Station upgrade
  • Dagoretti south Drainage upgrade
