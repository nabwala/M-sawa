Nyayo Stadium Renovation

✔ Completed

Project Overview

  • Budget: KES 120 Million
  • Timeline: January 2023 – September 2023
  • Location: Nairobi West
  • Lead Contractor: KenBuild Ltd.
  • Supervising Department: Nairobi County Sports Board

The Nyayo Stadium Renovation project aimed to modernize seating, lighting, drainage systems, and athlete facilities. It was launched as part of Nairobi County’s 2023 urban renewal plan, with full completion in September 2023. The revamped stadium now meets international hosting standards for athletics and football.

Leadership During Project Tenure

  • Governor: Hon. Sakaja Johnson
  • Member of Parliament (Westlands): Hon. Tim Wanyonyi
  • MCA (Nairobi West Ward): Hon. Mercy Ndunge
